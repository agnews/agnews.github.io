My Videos

December, 2020. Rough Draft "Cloud Bear Study". Inspired by the Google Image search "cloud bear". When you see the shape of a bear in a cloud, it effects the way you relate to real bears. To edit: I was trying to balance between didactic while leaving space for speculation. I was overly didactic, and explained the general concept too literally. In editing, I should remove some of the explanation.

February, 2020. The video’s focus is the Pirelli Tire Building. The first section montages the flowing motion of space in Pixar movies, intended to create a sense of Utopian wonder. The second section compares the social history of the Pirelli building to the idealistic writings of its architect, Marcel Breuer. The third section displays renderings of common-place words, clad in Pixar-inspired furs. The video is about the possibility of imaginary space versus its reality.

May, 2020. Comparison between the brutalist architecture of the Lily Acheson Wallace World of Birds and the social behaviors of a resident, Paprika. Maybe better as a sound piece: Paprika's voice projected into a concrete chamber.

September, 2020. Giant squids wash up on the shore and draft a fantasy of an undiscovered world. Giant squids become a symbol for human pathos.
